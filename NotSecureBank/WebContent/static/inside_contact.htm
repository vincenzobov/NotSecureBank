E-mail Security

Any inquiry you send to Not Secure Bank via our Contact Us page uses Secure Socket Layer (SSL) encryption. SSL helps to ensure that your personal information remains confidential.

To take advantage of SSL, however, you must have an SSL-compatible browser. Not Secure Bank recommends you use the following browsers:

  • Windows and Linux operating systems: Google Chrome 59.0.3071.86 or later, Mozilla Firefox 53.0.2 or later, and Microsoft Internet Explorer 11.0 or later.
  • macOS: Safari 10.1 or later, Google Chrome 59.0.3071.86 or later, Mozilla Firefox 53.0.2 or later.

Go to Google, Mozilla, Apple, or Microsoft for downloads.
