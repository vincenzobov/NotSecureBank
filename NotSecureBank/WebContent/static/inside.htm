Part of a Corporate Family

Not Secure Bank is a subsidiary of Not Secure, a multi-state holding company located in the heart of Massachusetts. Not Secure Bank has been serving Boston and surrounding communities for nearly 75 years. With nearly $20 billion in managed assets and over 7,000 employees, Not Secure Bank ranks as one of the fifty largest banks in the United States. Not Secure Bank serves over 6.6 million customers in all 50 states, with more than 90 banking locations in New England alone.
